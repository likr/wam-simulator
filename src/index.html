WAM Simulator

Whack-A-Mole (WAM) Model Simulator

The simulator solves a following differential equation: $$\frac{F(t)}{dt} = A(t) - B(t) F(t)$$ where, $$A(t) = a_0 + a_1 d(t)$$ $$B(t) = b_0 + b_1 d(t)$$

Notation Description
\(t\) Time (hour)
\(F(t)\) Mutation frequency at \(t\)
\(A(t)\) Effect of increasing mutations at \(t\)
\(B(t)\) Effect of decreasing mutations at \(t\)
\(a_0\) Increase rate of mutation in natural environment
\(a_1\) Increase rate of mutation correspnding to radiation dose
\(b_0\) Mutation repair rate in natural environment
\(b_1\) Mutation repair rate corresponding to radiation dose
\(d(t)\) Dose rate at \(t\) (Gy/hour)
